## [1.2.1] - 2026-03-28

### 🚀 Added (Genesis Hyper-Release)
- **TDOG Matter Compiler**: Introduced the core algorithmic engine for the Theory of Dynamic Object Generation. 
  - Supports Type_3 generation via Magneto-Rheological (MR) Fluid instantly hardening into structural bones.
  - Supports Type_2 generation via Dielectric Elastomers (DE) and Shape Memory Polymers (SMP) for localized 4D self-assembly.
- **The Ouroboros Protocol (`decompile_matter`)**: Implemented the ultimate zero-waste loop. Decompiles objects atom-by-atom when they exit the Haptic Horizon, deducting calculated entropy taxes.
- **Hyper-Environment Generator (`set_hyper_environment`)**: Shatters Earth's constants. Allows overriding physical laws within a strictly bound 2m×2m×2.4m H-SSSU cell (Temperature: -270℃ ~ 3000℃, Gravity: 0G ~ 100G).
- **0.2s Hardware Safety Fuse (`emergency_cutoff`)**: Hardcoded a non-bypassable safety cutoff protocol. Restores hyper-environments to 26℃, 1G, 101.3kPa safe baselines in under 0.2 seconds upon anomaly detection.
- **Bilingual Genesis Whitepaper**: Integrated the *S2 Hyper-TDOG & Hyper-Environment Genesis Whitepaper* (EN/CN) as the definitive guide for computational materials and extreme environmental simulation.

### 🔒 Security & OpenClaw Compliance
- Implemented sub-centimeter environmental firewalls via mmWave radar and infrared boundary simulations.
- Fully compliant with OpenClaw v1.0+ strict registry validations, including explicit `compat.pluginApi` and headless `configSchema` declarations.
- Sandboxed all thermodynamic and material states in local SQLite (`s2_tdog_genesis.db`).
